# How do I recover my username or reset my password?
## Recovering Your Username

If you have forgotten your username you can have a reminder sent to the email address associated with your account.

Either click on the **Recover username** link on the Sherpa website login page or you can navigate to the page directly using the link below.

<http://sherpa.escape-technology.com/username/recover>

![recover-your-username](/sherpa-docs/assets/images/recover-your-username.png)

## Recovering Your Password

If you have forgotten your password name you will have to reset it. To begin the process either click on the **Recover password** link on the Sherpa website login page or navigate to the page directly using the link below.

<http://sherpa.escape-technology.com/password/reset>

![recover-yopur-password](/sherpa-docs/assets/images/recover-yopur-password.png)

Once you have submitted the form we will send a reset link to your email address. Clicking on the reset link will take you to a form where you can update your password to one you can remember.

![reset-your-password](/sherpa-docs/assets/images/reset-your-password.png)

The password you choose must meet the following requirements:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least 1 uppercase letter
- Contain at least 1 lowercase letter
- Contain at least 1 number
- Contain at least 1 special character
- Not have a period (full stop) as the final character
